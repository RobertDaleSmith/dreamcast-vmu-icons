  All icons here can be downloaded with Dreamcast. If you would like to download these icons with your PC and put them on your 2/4/8/16 meg memory card /w PC link. Right click on the link to the icon and pick "Copy Shortcut" and right click on the address bar and pick "Paste". Then change the file extension from VMI to VMS(ex.  icon.vmi to icon.vms) and press enter to download it. Then use the program called "DCI_VMI" from the tools section on the menu, to convert the VMS file to a DCI file.
